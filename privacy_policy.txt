Privacy Policy for EZ Home Inspection

Effective Date: August 7, 2024

Introduction

EZ Home Inspection (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data when you use our application.

Information We Collect

EZ Home Inspection does not store any user data. When you use our application, we only process the home inspection reports you upload to provide you with analysis results.

How We Use Information

	1.	Processing Reports: The home inspection reports you upload are converted into text and sent to a third-party analysis API.
	2.	Returning Results: The analysis results are then returned to you through our application.

Third-Party API

The third-party analysis API we use also does not store any of the data it processes. It only analyzes the data and returns the results to our application.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data during transmission. However, since we do not store any user data, there is no risk of data being stored or compromised on our end.

Your Rights

As we do not collect or store any personal data, you do not need to request access, update, or delete any personal information.
